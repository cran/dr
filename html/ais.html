Australian athletes data

Description

Data on 102 male and 100 female athletes collected at the Australian Institute of Sport, courtesy of Richard Telford and Ross Cunningham.

Format

This data frame contains the following columns:

Sex
(0 = male or 1 = female)
Ht
height (cm)
Wt
weight (kg)
LBM
lean body mass
RCC
red cell count
WCC
white cell count
Hc
Hematocrit
Hg
Hemoglobin

Details

Source

R. D. Cook and S. Weisberg (1999). Applied Statistics Including Computing and Graphics. New York: Wiley.
